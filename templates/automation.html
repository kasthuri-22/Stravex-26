Smart Automation

Designing AI-driven, sensor-based systems that automate real-world processes to improve safety and efficiency.

Problem Statements

Choose Your Challenge

Build intelligent automation systems that solve real, high-impact challenges.

01
Computer Vision / Safety

Automated Smart Construction Site Safety Monitor

Construction sites often face safety violations such as workers not wearing helmets or safety gear, leading to accidents.

Problem Statement

Build a computer vision system that detects whether workers are wearing mandatory safety gear and alerts supervisors in real time.

02
AI / Career

AI-Based Smart Job Role Recommendation System

Students struggle to choose suitable career paths due to lack of personalized guidance.

Problem Statement

Develop an AI model that analyzes students' skills, interests, and academic performance to recommend suitable job roles.

03
IoT / Flood Detection

Automated Smart Street Flood Detection System

Urban flooding disrupts traffic and public safety. Authorities often receive delayed information.

Problem Statement

Design a real-time flood detection and prediction system to alert authorities and the public.

04
AI / Traffic

Smart Dynamic Traffic Signal Control System

Traditional traffic signals use fixed timers and do not adapt to real-time traffic conditions.

Problem Statement

Create an intelligent traffic signal system that adjusts signal timing based on traffic density.

05
IoT / Energy

Smart Energy Monitoring System

High energy wastage in homes and institutions increases electricity bills and carbon emissions.

Problem Statement

Build a system to monitor appliance-level energy consumption and suggest energy-saving measures.

06
Hardware / Security

Smart AI-Based Security Fence Monitoring System

Critical areas such as agricultural lands, borders, and restricted zones need secure perimeter monitoring.

Problem Statement

Develop a hardware-based intelligent fence system that detects intrusion, tampering, or unauthorized access.

07
AI / Disaster

Automated Smart Disaster Evacuation Assistant

During disasters, people panic and lack proper evacuation guidance.

Problem Statement

Design a system that provides real-time safe evacuation routes during emergencies.

08
RFID / Healthcare

Smart Inventory Expiry Management for Pharmacies

Expired medicines cause health risks and financial losses.

Problem Statement

Create a barcode/RFID-based system to track medicine expiry dates and prevent expired stock usage.

09
AI / CCTV

AI-Powered Smart Facial Recognition CCTV System

Traditional CCTV systems require manual monitoring and delay threat detection.

Problem Statement

Develop an AI-based CCTV system that detects unknown persons and alerts homeowners instantly.

10
IoT / Labs

IoT-Based Smart Monitoring for Skill Training Labs

Vocational training labs face equipment breakdowns, poor monitoring, and unsafe conditions.

Problem Statement

Build a centralized IoT system to monitor machine health and environmental safety in labs.
