Track 01

Campus Micro-Systems

Identifying and solving overlooked operational, logistical, and convenience challenges on a college campus.

10 Problem Statements
Max 5 Members
19 March 2026
Problem Statements

Choose Your Challenge

Read each problem carefully and build an intelligent, feasible solution.

01
Library / Search

Library Book Search and Availability System

In many college libraries, students spend a lot of time manually searching for books. Sometimes books are unavailable, misplaced, or already issued, which causes confusion and time wastage.

Problem Statement

Develop a simple application that allows users to search books and check their availability status.

02
Energy / Monitoring

Electricity Consumption Monitoring System

Colleges often record electricity consumption manually without proper tracking or analysis. This makes it difficult to monitor monthly usage and identify areas of excessive power consumption.

Problem Statement

Design a system where daily electricity meter readings can be entered manually and monthly consumption can be monitored using charts.

03
Transport / Tracking

College Bus Tracking System

Students often wait for college buses without knowing the exact arrival time or current location. This leads to unnecessary waiting and inconvenience.

Problem Statement

Develop a simple system where the bus driver can update the current location/status, and students can view it in real time.

04
Scheduling / Reminders

Smart Timetable and Reminder Application

Students sometimes miss classes or forget schedule changes due to lack of proper reminders. Printed timetables are not dynamic and do not provide notifications.

Problem Statement

Build an application that displays the class timetable and sends reminders for upcoming classes.

05
Lost & Found

Lost and Found Management Portal

When students lose personal belongings on campus, there is no centralized system to report or search for lost items. This reduces the chances of recovery.

Problem Statement

Create an online portal where students can report lost or found items and search for matching entries.

06
Infrastructure / Parking

Smart Parking Slot Management System

Students and staff face difficulty finding available parking spaces inside the campus, leading to congestion and time loss. There is no system to display parking availability.

Problem Statement

Develop a basic system that displays available and occupied parking slots in real time (manual update version).

07
Canteen / Pre-Order

Digital Canteen Pre-Order System

During break times, long queues form in the college canteen, causing delays and inconvenience. There is no system for advance ordering.

Problem Statement

Design a web or mobile-based system that allows students to view the menu, place pre-orders, and receive a token number for pickup.

08
Administration / Complaints

Online Complaint Management System

Students face issues related to infrastructure, facilities, or academics but often do not have a proper channel to register and track complaints. Manual complaint systems lack transparency.

Problem Statement

Create a centralized digital platform where students can submit complaints and track their status, and administrators can manage and update them.

09
Examination / Allocation

Automatic Classroom and Seat Allocation System

Classroom and seating arrangements are often done manually, especially during exams, which can lead to confusion and errors.

Problem Statement

Develop a simple application that automatically assigns students to classrooms and seats using their register numbers or department details.

10
Academic / Peer Support

Peer Learning Support Platform

Many students struggle in certain subjects but hesitate to ask for help. There is no structured platform in the campus to connect students who need academic support with those who can provide it.

Problem Statement

Develop a platform where students can list the subjects or topics they need help with and connect with peers who are willing to assist them.
