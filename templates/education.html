Track 04

Smart Education

Reimagining education through AI, automation, and data-driven platforms that personalise learning outcomes.

10 Problem Statements
Max 5 Members
19 March 2026
Problem Statements

Choose Your Challenge

Build intelligent education systems that personalise learning and reduce friction.

01
AI / Placement

AI-Based Placement Preparation System

Preparing for campus placements is stressful for many college students. Most students practice without knowing their weak areas and do not receive proper mock interview feedback.

Problem Statement

Build an AI-based system that provides personalized aptitude practice, coding tests, and AI-powered mock interviews with detailed feedback to help students prepare effectively for campus placements.

02
AI / Internships

Smart Internship & Industry Matching Platform

Students find it hard to get internships that match their skills and interests. Many apply without knowing if they are suitable. Companies also struggle to find the right students.

Problem Statement

Build an AI platform that matches students with internships based on their skills, marks, and interests.

03
AI / Research

Digital Research Paper Recommendation System

Students spend too much time searching for research papers and often find irrelevant results. It is difficult to choose the correct and useful papers.

Problem Statement

Build an AI system that suggests suitable research papers based on subject and keywords.

04
ML / Analytics

Smart Academic Performance Prediction System

Some students score low because no one notices their weak performance early. Colleges usually identify problems only after exams.

Problem Statement

Build a system that predicts student performance using attendance and marks and gives early warning alerts.

05
AI / Planning

Smart Time Management System for College Students

Students struggle to manage studies, projects, internships, and activities. Poor planning causes stress and missed deadlines.

Problem Statement

Build an AI planner that helps students create daily schedules and manage their time properly.

06
AI / Skills

Skill Gap Analysis Platform

Many students do not know what skills they are missing for jobs. They realize it only during placements.

Problem Statement

Build an AI system that checks student skills and suggests what they need to learn to improve.

07
AI / Examination

Automated Exam Paper Generation System

Teachers spend a lot of time preparing question papers. It is hard to make sure all topics are covered properly.

Problem Statement

Build a system that automatically creates question papers with proper syllabus coverage and balanced difficulty.

08
CV / Attendance

Smart Attendance System using Face Recognition

Manual attendance takes time and sometimes students mark attendance for others. Keeping records on paper is difficult.

Problem Statement

Build a face recognition system that automatically marks attendance and stores it digitally.

09
AI / Networking

AI-Based Alumni Networking Platform

Students do not have a proper way to connect with alumni for guidance and career advice.

Problem Statement

Build an AI platform that connects students with alumni based on similar career interests.

10
AI / Integrity

Smart Assignment Submission & Plagiarism Checker

Submitting assignments manually is difficult to manage, and copying is common. Teachers find it hard to check originality.

Problem Statement

Build an online system where students can submit assignments and check for plagiarism automatically.
