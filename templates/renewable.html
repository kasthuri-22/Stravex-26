Track 02

Resource Efficiency & Micro Sustainability

Building intelligent, low-cost solutions that reduce waste and optimize resource consumption at a micro scale.

10 Problem Statements
Max 5 Members
19 March 2026
Problem Statements

Choose Your Challenge

Select any one problem and propose a low-cost, scalable, intelligent approach.

01
IoT / Water

Smart Water Leakage Detection System

Water wastage due to undetected leaks is a significant problem in homes, hostels, and offices. Users are often unaware of excessive consumption until they receive a large utility bill.

Problem Statement

Design a low-cost IoT-based system that detects water leakage and monitors excessive water usage, providing real-time alerts and usage analytics to reduce wastage.

02
AI / Waste

AI-Powered Waste Classification System

Improper waste segregation at the household level reduces recycling efficiency and increases landfill burden. Manual sorting is inconsistent and unreliable across communities.

Problem Statement

Develop an AI-powered mobile or camera-based system that automatically classifies household waste into recyclable, compostable, and hazardous categories to improve micro-level waste management.

03
Energy / Grid

Smart Micro-Grid Energy Optimizer

Campuses and small communities struggle to balance renewable energy generation with storage and consumption. Existing systems are often expensive and not scalable to smaller institutions.

Problem Statement

Design a smart micro-grid system for campuses or small communities that optimizes energy generation, storage, and consumption using renewable sources.

04
Water / Monitoring

Real-Time Abnormal Water Consumption Detector

Residential and institutional buildings consume water at irregular rates with no mechanism to flag abnormal usage. Leakages often go undetected for long periods causing significant wastage.

Problem Statement

Develop a real-time water monitoring system that detects abnormal consumption patterns and leakage in residential or institutional buildings using sensor data and pattern analysis.

05
Resource Sharing

Student Resource Sharing & Reuse Platform

Students frequently purchase new books, tools, and electronics that are discarded after use, creating unnecessary waste and financial burden. There is no structured system for sharing or reusing.

Problem Statement

Build a digital platform that enables students to share, reuse, or exchange resources such as books, tools, electronics, and materials to reduce consumption and waste.

06
Agriculture / IoT

Low-Cost Smart Irrigation System

Traditional irrigation methods result in overwatering or underwatering of crops. Farmers lack real-time data on soil conditions and upcoming weather to make informed irrigation decisions.

Problem Statement

Develop a low-cost smart irrigation system using soil moisture sensors and weather prediction data to optimize water usage and reduce agricultural water wastage.

07
Energy / Buildings

Smart Academic Building Energy Optimizer

Academic buildings consume significant amounts of electricity with no system to monitor or optimize usage. Lights and appliances remain on in unused rooms, wasting energy continuously.

Problem Statement

Design a smart system that monitors and optimizes electricity usage in academic buildings by analyzing real-time consumption patterns and recommending energy-saving actions.

08
Transport / Eco

Eco-Efficient Campus Transport Management

Campus transportation systems often run on fixed schedules regardless of occupancy, leading to unnecessary fuel consumption and carbon emissions. No optimization for demand or routing exists.

Problem Statement

Develop a smart transport management system that minimizes fuel usage and carbon emissions for campus transportation through route optimization and real-time occupancy monitoring.

09
Architecture / Tool

Building Sustainability Evaluation Tool

Architects and builders lack software tools to assess building layouts for natural lighting efficiency, ventilation quality, and material optimization before construction begins.

Problem Statement

Create a software tool that evaluates building layouts for natural lighting, ventilation efficiency, and material optimization to improve micro-sustainability during the design phase.

10
Construction / Analytics

Smart Material Usage Analyzer for Construction

Small-scale construction and renovation projects frequently over-order materials, leading to significant waste and excess resource consumption with no data-driven planning support.

Problem Statement

Develop a digital solution that analyzes material usage in small-scale construction or renovation projects and suggests optimized material planning to reduce waste and excess resource consumption.
