Track 05

Inclusive & Accessible Systems

Engineering low-cost, human-centred solutions that empower individuals with disabilities.

10 Problem Statements
Max 5 Members
19 March 2026
Problem Statements

Choose Your Challenge

Design for empathy — build systems that make the world more accessible for everyone.

01
Assistive AI

Real-Time Sign Language Translation Platform

Many deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals face communication barriers during live conversations. There is a lack of accessible, real-time systems that seamlessly translate sign language into spoken language and text, and vice versa.

Problem Statement

Create a real-time AI-powered platform that recognizes sign language through vision-based models and converts it into text or speech, while also translating spoken language into sign language during live interactions.

02
Smart Accessibility

Indoor Navigation Assistance System

Visually impaired individuals often struggle to navigate complex indoor environments such as colleges, hospitals, and public buildings due to the absence of structured guidance systems.

Problem Statement

Develop an intelligent indoor navigation system using computer vision, Bluetooth beacons, or ultrasonic sensors that delivers real-time audio guidance and obstacle awareness for visually impaired users.

03
Inclusive Learning

Physically Optimized Classroom Design

Students with dyslexia, ADHD, or autism often experience difficulty concentrating in conventional classroom environments that lack sensory and structural accommodations.

Problem Statement

Design a physically optimized classroom environment that integrates adaptive lighting, improved acoustics, flexible seating arrangements, and sensory regulation zones to enhance focus, comfort, and learning outcomes.

04
Secure Assistive Voting

Independent Digital Voting Interface

Many individuals with physical or sensory disabilities face challenges in casting votes independently and privately due to inaccessible voting mechanisms.

Problem Statement

Engineer a secure, voice-assisted and gesture-controlled digital voting interface that ensures full independence, privacy, and accessibility during the voting process.

05
Mental Health Accessibility

AAC-Based Emotional Support Companion

Individuals with speech or motor impairments often find it difficult to express emotions or seek help, limiting their access to mental health support.

Problem Statement

Implement an AAC-based mental health companion application that utilizes symbol boards, eye-tracking, or switch access to help users communicate emotions and receive personalized support.

06
Affordable Mobility

Low-Cost Assistive Mobility Device

Mobility-impaired students face challenges navigating uneven terrains, staircases, and narrow corridors within campus environments due to limited affordable assistive solutions.

Problem Statement

Innovate a low-cost, manually operable assistive device or wheelchair attachment that enhances independent mobility across uneven surfaces, stairs, and confined spaces.

07
Inclusive Career

Fully Accessible Hiring System

Job seekers with disabilities often encounter barriers in digital hiring processes due to non-adaptive interfaces and limited assessment formats.

Problem Statement

Develop a fully accessible hiring platform that integrates adaptive user interfaces, AI-powered interview tools supporting text, sign language, and voice formats, along with disability-inclusive skill assessments.

08
Emergency Preparedness

Disability-Centered Evacuation Framework

Emergency evacuation systems in public buildings are often not designed to accommodate individuals with physical or sensory disabilities.

Problem Statement

Formulate a structural and mechanical emergency evacuation plan incorporating evacuation chairs, tactile floor guides, vibration-based alert panels, and other physical aids to ensure safe evacuation for disabled individuals.

09
Accessible Medical Comm.

Real-Time Medical Document Simplification System

Medical documents are often complex and difficult to understand, creating barriers to informed consent for individuals with cognitive, linguistic, or sensory challenges.

Problem Statement

Design a system that transforms medical documents into plain language, audio formats, pictograms, or regional languages in real time to ensure accessible understanding and informed decision-making.

10
Public Transport

Retrofittable Inclusive Transport Infrastructure

Public transport systems frequently lack affordable and easily implementable accessibility solutions, restricting mobility for disabled individuals.

Problem Statement

Revolutionize public transport accessibility by designing low-cost ramp systems, tactile pathways, and ergonomic boarding aids that can be retrofitted into existing buses and train stations without major structural reconstruction.
